# StrategyPMO — SupaDemo Recording Script

**Total Duration:** ~8-10 minutes  
**Presenter:** Product owner or demo lead  
**Audience:** Senior executives, government clients, IT stakeholders

---

## Pre-Recording Setup
- Login as **Admin** role (sees all features)
- Ensure demo data is loaded (NSA Corporate Strategy 2025-2030)
- Browser: Full screen, no bookmarks bar, dark mode OFF
- Resolution: 1920×1080

---

## SCENE 1: Strategy Map (First Impression)
**Click:** Sidebar → **Strategy Map** (first item)

**Narration:**
> "Welcome to StrategyPMO. This is the Strategy Map — your programme's single source of truth. At a glance you see the vision, mission, strategic pillars, and how initiatives roll up into the overall strategy. Each pillar shows its progress, and you can click through to drill into any area."

**Actions:**
1. Hover over a pillar card to show the progress tooltip
2. Click "Edit" on the vision/mission to show it's configurable

**Duration:** 45 seconds

---

## SCENE 2: Programme Dashboard
**Click:** Sidebar → **Dashboard**

**Narration:**
> "The Dashboard gives executives a real-time overview. Strategy progress, initiative count, project count, and budget utilisation — all computed automatically from the ground up. The Department Health chart shows each department's project status breakdown."

**Actions:**
1. Point to the 4 metric cards (Progress, Initiatives, Projects, Budget)
2. Scroll to Department Health stacked bars
3. Scroll to the project status pie chart (numbers embedded inline)
4. Click **Strategic Pillars** tab to show pillar-level drill-down
5. Click **Export PDF** button (show the report generates with timestamp)

**Duration:** 90 seconds

---

## SCENE 3: My Tasks (PM's Daily View)
**Click:** Sidebar → **My Tasks**

**Narration:**
> "Every user sees their personalised task list. Critical items at the top — overdue milestones, approval requests. Tasks are categorised: Milestones, Actions, Weekly Reports, KPIs, Risks, and Project Health. Click any filter to focus."

**Actions:**
1. Point to the priority sections (Critical, High, Medium)
2. Click the **Milestones** category filter chip
3. Click **Clear** to reset
4. Click a task card to navigate to the project

**Duration:** 45 seconds

---

## SCENE 4: Projects — Three Views
**Click:** Sidebar → **Projects**

### 4a: Grouped View
**Narration:**
> "Projects are grouped by pillar and initiative — the full strategy hierarchy. Each project shows progress, dates, budget, milestones, and owner."

**Actions:**
1. Expand a pillar, then an initiative to show projects
2. Point to a project row showing progress bar, status badge, weight

### 4b: Flat Projects View
**Click:** Toggle → **Projects** button (middle)

**Narration:**
> "Switch to the flat view for a clean alphabetical list. Hover the hierarchy icon to see which pillar and initiative each project belongs to."

**Actions:**
1. Hover the coloured Layers icon on a project to show the hierarchy popover
2. Show the multi-select filter dropdowns (Pillar, Department, Status, Phase)
3. Select two statuses to filter, then click **Clear**

### 4c: Gantt Chart
**Click:** Toggle → **Gantt** button

**Narration:**
> "The Gantt view shows project timelines with milestones as diamond markers. Scroll horizontally using the top scrollbar. Groups start collapsed — click to expand."

**Actions:**
1. Scroll using the top scrollbar
2. Click "Expand All" button
3. Click a project bar to show the hover card

**Duration:** 90 seconds total

---

## SCENE 5: Project Deep Dive
**Click:** Click **Deep Dive** (telescope icon) on any project

**Narration:**
> "The deep dive is the complete project view. Owner badge at the top, objectives, description, planned vs actual dates, budget breakdown with CAPEX/OPEX split, and milestone progress."

**Actions:**
1. Point to the **Project Owner** blue badge
2. Scroll to **Description & Objectives** section
3. Point to the planned dates and budget cards
4. Click **Milestones** tab — show the milestone table with start dates, due dates, weights, progress
5. Click an inline weight edit to show it's editable
6. Click **Risks & Actions** tab — show risk cards and action items
7. Click **Reports** tab — show weekly report submission
8. Click **Discussion** tab — show @mention comments

**Duration:** 90 seconds

---

## SCENE 6: KPIs
**Click:** Sidebar → **KPIs**

**Narration:**
> "Strategic and Operational KPIs grouped by pillar. Each shows baseline, target, actual, and achievement percentage. Filter by pillar to focus."

**Actions:**
1. Click a pillar filter chip to filter
2. Click a KPI row to open the **KPI Deep Dive** modal
3. In the modal: point to the owner badge, target rationale, multi-year table
4. Show the measurement form (admin/owner only)
5. Close the modal

**Duration:** 60 seconds

---

## SCENE 7: Risks & Issues
**Click:** Sidebar → **Risks & Issues**

**Narration:**
> "All programme risks with a heat map showing probability vs impact. Filter by severity. When a risk materialises, convert it to an Issue with one click."

**Actions:**
1. Show the risk heat map at the top
2. Scroll to the risk cards
3. Click a risk to show details
4. Point to the "Convert to Issue" button

**Duration:** 45 seconds

---

## SCENE 8: Analytics — Intelligence Engines
**Click:** Sidebar → **Analytics** (under Configuration section)

### 8a: Overview
**Narration:**
> "The analytics engine runs 11 intelligence modules. The overview shows delay predictions, budget forecasts, stakeholder alerts, and EVM metrics — all computed in real time."

### 8b: Earned Value Management
**Click:** Tab → **Earned Value**

**Narration:**
> "EVM shows cost and schedule performance for every project. Hover column headers for plain-English explanations. CPI below 1 means over budget, SPI below 1 means behind schedule."

**Actions:** Hover a help icon to show the tooltip

### 8c: Stakeholder Intel
**Click:** Tab → **Stakeholder Intel**

**Narration:**
> "The stakeholder engine identifies bottlenecks: approval delays, missing weekly reports, inactive PMs. Filter by category or severity. Click a PM's name to go to their project, or hit Remind to send an email."

**Actions:**
1. Click the **Missing Weekly Reports** category filter
2. Click the **Remind** button on one card
3. Click **Clear** to reset

### 8d: Scenario Simulation
**Click:** Tab → **Scenarios**

**Narration:**
> "What-if analysis. Select a project, choose Delay, Cancel, or Cut Budget. The engine simulates the impact on milestone progress, initiative, pillar, and programme levels."

**Actions:**
1. Select a project → **Delay Project** → 90 days → **Simulate**
2. Point to the Progress Shortfall card (planned vs simulated)
3. Point to the Strategy Cascade Impact card
4. Scroll to the milestone-by-milestone breakdown
5. Switch to **Cancel Project** → Simulate → show the Strategy Impact, Financial Impact, What Gets Cancelled cards

**Duration:** 120 seconds total

---

## SCENE 9: Configuration (Admin)
**Click:** Sidebar → **Configuration** (gear icon at bottom)

**Narration:**
> "Admins configure thresholds, phase gate weights, email schedules, currency, and user management — all from one page."

**Actions:**
1. Show the phase gate weight sliders
2. Scroll to **Task Reminders** section — show the automated email scheduler toggle and frequency selector
3. Scroll to **Weekly Report Submission Reminders** — show the separate section with upcoming + overdue config
4. Scroll to **Currency** selector
5. Show the **User Management** section with role assignment and block/unblock

**Duration:** 60 seconds

---

## SCENE 10: Monitoring
**Click:** Sidebar → **Monitoring**

**Narration:**
> "Monitoring has three tabs: Alerts for programme-wide warnings, Activity Log for a complete audit trail of every action, and Progress Proof for milestone evidence review."

**Actions:**
1. Show **Alerts** tab with alert cards
2. Click **Activity Log** tab — show the timeline of actions
3. Click **Progress Proof** tab — show the milestone evidence review grid

---

## SCENE 11: Reports (PDF & PPTX)

**Narration:**
> "Generate executive reports with one click. PDF includes cover page with timestamp, programme overview, department breakdown, project portfolio, budget & KPIs, risk heat map, and escalations. PPTX version is presentation-ready."

**Actions:**
1. Download the PDF
2. Open it — show the cover page with "Generated: [date] at [time]"
3. Scroll through 2-3 pages showing charts and tables
4. Go back and click **Export PPTX** — show it downloads

**Duration:** 45 seconds

---

## SCENE 12: Import & Bulk Operations
**Click:** Sidebar → **Import Data**

**Narration:**
> "Import your strategy data from Excel in seconds. Download the template, fill it in, upload, review the parsed data, and confirm. The AI-powered parser maps columns automatically."

**Actions:**
1. Click **Download Template**
2. Show the upload area
3. (Optional) Show a pre-uploaded analysis result

**Duration:** 30 seconds

## Tips for SupaDemo Recording

1. **Click slowly** — SupaDemo captures each click as a step
2. **Hover before clicking** — gives the viewer context
3. **Use the search/command palette** (Cmd+K) at least once to show it exists
4. **Show the notification bell** — hover to show in-app notifications
5. **Resize the sidebar** — click the collapse button to show it works
6. **Don't show the login page** — start already logged in
7. **Add hotspot annotations** in SupaDemo's editor for key features
8. **Set step transitions** to 0.5s for smooth playback
